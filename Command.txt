help - shows help menu.

Make-Item <directory_name> - makes directory.

Make-Item--verbose <directory_name> - shows making directory.

Make-Item--help - shows mkdir help.

Make-Item--version - shows version.

Remove-Item <directory_name> - removes directory.

Remove-Item--verbose <directory_name> - shows removing directory.

Remove-Item--help - shows rmdir help.

Remove-Item--version - shows version.

Set-Location <directory_name> - changes to specified directory.

cls or clear - clears screen.

Get-ChildItem - shows files.

exit - exits PowerShell.
